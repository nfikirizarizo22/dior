About Me

I've been captivated by the way rhythm can evoke feelings since I was five years old, when I struck a drum for the first time. I've given drumming my whole attention over the past 2 years, experimenting with a variety of styles and collaborating with many artists that have helped in my artistic development. My level was changed by playing in a local band, which taught me the value of collaboration and engaging the audience during live performances. Sharing my enthusiasm and expertise with younger drummers is something else I enjoy doing. In the future, I can't wait to pursue my studies in music in a nurturing setting that encourages innovation and teamwork while pushing me to become a better drummer. For me, playing the drums is more than just a hobby; it's an essential way to express my feelings.
